## â“ Common Questions

### "What if I need equipment for multiple days?"
Reserve consecutive blocks across multiple days. Remember the 3-block weekly limit per category.

### "Can I extend my reservation?"
Only if no one else has reserved that equipment for the next block. Check with staff.

### "What if equipment is damaged?"
Report it IMMEDIATELY to staff. Don't try to fix it yourself. You won't be charged for normal wear and tear or damage you didn't cause.

### "I can't find the equipment I need"
- Try different search terms
- Check if it's filtered out by category
- Ask staff if similar equipment is available

### "My reservation disappeared!"
Check your university email - you might have received a cancellation notice. Contact the equipment office.

### "Can I reserve equipment for someone else?"
No. Only the person picking up can make the reservation. This is for accountability.
